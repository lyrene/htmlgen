WERpapers - Open Access Repository

WER - Workshop on Requirements Engineering

WERpapers is an open-access repository, that catalogs proceedings and articles spanning across every edition of the Requirements Engineering Workshop (WER). This workshop has been held annually since 1998. Its purpose is to promote meaningful discussions among English, Portuguese, and Spanish speakers, fostering a collaborative environment to develop cutting-edge solutions within the realm of requirements engineering.

WERpapers was originally created in 2005 at DI - PUC-Rio. The ISSN (2675-0066) was registered by PUC-Rio Editora, which also provided DOIs for several years between 1998 and 2018. Since 2024, WERpapers has been operated and maintained by Dimap - UFRN. Editora Even3 has provided DOIs since 2019. Werpapers articles are indexed in DBLP and GoogleScholar, and for some years they are also indexed in Scopus. In order to improve indexing, this site is also available in a pure HTML version.

To access the knowledge encapsulated in the workshop's proceedings, navigate through the comprehensive list below or employ the search form.
